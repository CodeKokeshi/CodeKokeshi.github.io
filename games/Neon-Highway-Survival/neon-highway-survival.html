About the Game

Neon Highway Survival is a fast-paced top-down shooter where you must survive waves of enemies in a neon-lit dystopian world.

Gameplay Features

  • Drive and commandeer vehicles for extra speed, protection, and firepower
  • Face increasingly difficult waves of enemies
  • Level up and choose from powerful upgrades including the Arc Spitter Array (multishot) and Sentinel Drone (auto-targeting companion)
  • Pick timed "flash card" boosts every 30 seconds for temporary buffs with trade-offs
  • Navigate around obstacles and use terrain strategically
  • Survive as long as possible and beat your high score

Strategy Tips

  • Vehicles provide significant advantages but can be destroyed
  • Eliminating enemies awards XP for permanent upgrades
  • Choose upgrades that complement your playstyle
  • Use obstacles to block enemy fire and create chokepoints
  • Some upgrades grant piercing shots that can hit multiple enemies

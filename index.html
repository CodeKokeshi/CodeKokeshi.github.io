About Me

Hi! I'm CodeKokeshi, a game developer, programmer, artist, and mod creator.

I started my journey in game development with RPG Maker, moved to Unity, and eventually found my home in Godot.

As a programmer, I work on web development and software development, specializing in Lenet-5 CNN and machine learning applications.

My artistic skills span SVG art, pixel art, and digital art, with animation capabilities across all these mediums.

I'm also an active mod maker, focusing primarily on creating quality-of-life mods for Stardew Valley.
